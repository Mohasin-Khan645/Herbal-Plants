About the Project

Herbal Guardian promotes herbal awareness, disease identification, and AI-driven guidance using Google Gemini.

Sustainable Goals

  • Encourage herbal education and responsible usage.
  • Promote home-grown herbal gardens.
  • Support community interest in herbal sustainability.

Technology

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • UI: Bootstrap 5
  • AI: Google Gemini for text generation
  • PWA: Offline support via Service Worker
